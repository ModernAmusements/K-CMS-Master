Description: As part of Nike's Tech Collective I was asked to create a short animation to respond to the new TechPack clothing range. I focussed on technology and innovative fabrics through a mixture of high resolution scanning, formula-based image making and 3D animation. The work has gone up in a special exhibition in the NikeTown store throughout late 2018.
